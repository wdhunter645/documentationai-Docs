## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before you begin">
You need a valid email address and a modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. No software installation required.
</Callout>

## Sign up for an account

Get started by creating your Bill Hunter account. The process takes less than 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the signup page" icon="user-plus">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://billhunter.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your email address, create a password, and complete any verification steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Bill Hunter and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create your first documentation space

Once logged in, create a space to organize your docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the dashboard" icon="home">
    After login, you land on the dashboard at `https://app.billhunter.com/dashboard`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new space" icon="plus">
    Click the `+ New Space` button in the top right. Enter a name like "My Project Docs" and select a template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize settings" icon="settings">
    Choose visibility (public, private, or workspace) and brand color (`#3B82F6` for Bill Hunter blue).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Import existing documents

Bill Hunter supports seamless imports from various sources.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Markdown Files" icon="file-text">
    Upload `.md` or `.mdx` files directly.
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Drag and drop">
        In your space, drag files into the import area.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select folder structure">
        Preserve your folder hierarchy for organized docs.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google Docs" icon="file">
    Connect your Google account for one-click import.
    
````markdown
1. Click "Import from Google Docs"
2. Authorize Bill Hunter
3. Select docs to import
````

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="GitHub Repo" icon="github">
    Import entire repos as living documentation.
    
    Use this URL format: `https://github.com/yourusername/your-repo`
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Basic navigation and interface tour

The Bill Hunter interface is intuitive:

- **Sidebar**: Spaces, pages, and search
- **Editor**: Real-time MDX editing with live preview
- **Top bar**: Publish, share, and settings

<Expandable title="Advanced navigation tips" default-open="false">
Use keyboard shortcuts like `Cmd/Ctrl + K` for quick search and `Cmd/Ctrl + S` to save drafts.
</Expandable>

## Save and publish your first page

Create a sample page to test.

<CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Markdown">
````mdx
## Welcome to My Docs

This is your first page.

- Edit in real-time
- Preview changes instantly
````

````markdown
# Welcome to My Docs

This is your first page.

- Edit in real-time
- Preview changes instantly
````
</CodeGroup>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new page" icon="file-plus">
    In your space, click `+ New Page`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add content" icon="edit">
    Paste the sample above into the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="upload">
    Click `Publish` to make it live. Share the URL: `https://docs.billhunter.com/yourspace/welcome`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="success">
Congratulations! You've set up Bill Hunter and published your first page. Explore more features next.
</Callout>
