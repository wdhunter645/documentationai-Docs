<Update label="2024-10-15" description="v1.2.0" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>

- Introduced comment threads with `@mentions` support

## Report Issues and Provide Feedback

Help improve Bill Hunter by reporting bugs or suggesting features.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search Existing Issues" icon="search">
    Check the [GitHub issues](https://github.com/your-org/bill-hunter/issues) page first to avoid duplicates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create New Issue" icon="plus">
    Click "New Issue" and provide:
    
    - Clear title
    - Steps to reproduce
    - Expected vs. actual behavior
    - Browser/environment details

  <Step title="Submit Feedback" icon="thumbs-up">
