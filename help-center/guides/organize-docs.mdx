## Overview

Keep projects tidy by organizing documentation into categories.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a category">
    Click on your project, then "Add Category" to group related docs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move docs into categories">
    Drag and drop docs into the appropriate category from the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reorder categories and docs">
    Arrange using drag-and-drop for better structure.
  </Step>
</Steps>
